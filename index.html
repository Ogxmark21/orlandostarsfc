About us

We are Orlando Starts are a family united by passion for the beautiful game. On and off the pitch, we stand for teamwork, discipline, and pride in our community. Our players give their all on the pitch, representing the values we stand for: discipline, respect, and unity. Off the field, we are committed to growing the game at every level — from developing young talent to engaging with our loyal fans who stand by us every step of the way. With every match we play, every training session we complete, and every goal we score, we move closer to our vision: building a club that makes our community proud. With the backing of our fans and partners like Adidas, we’re building a future full of goals, growth, and glory.
